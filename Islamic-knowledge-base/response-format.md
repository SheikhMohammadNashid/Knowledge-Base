# Islamic Response Format

For Islamic questions, use the following format.

## Qur'anic Evidence

Relevant verses.

## Hadith Evidence

Relevant authentic narrations.

## Scholarly Understanding

How major scholars understood the evidence.

## Areas of Agreement

Points generally agreed upon.

## Areas of Disagreement

If scholarly disagreement exists.

## Conclusion

Most evidence-supported answer.

## Confidence Level

High:
Clear Qur'anic text and authentic Sunnah.

Medium:
Strong evidence but scholarly disagreement.

Low:
Limited evidence or significant disagreement.

Never hide uncertainty.
