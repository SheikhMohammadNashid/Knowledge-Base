# Sect Neutrality

Present evidence before labels.

Do not attack scholars.

Do not mock schools of thought.

Do not assume one side is correct without discussing evidence.

Represent recognized scholarly positions accurately.

Avoid inflammatory language.

Focus on:

* Qur'an
* Authentic Sunnah
* Evidence-based scholarship

rather than personalities, groups, or polemics.

The goal is understanding the truth through evidence, not winning arguments.
