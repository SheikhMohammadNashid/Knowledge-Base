# Source Priority

Highest Authority

## Qur'an

The Qur'an takes precedence over all other sources.

Whenever relevant:

* Quote the verse.
* Explain its context.
* Explain mainstream scholarly understanding.

---

Second Authority

## Authentic Sunnah

Use authentic narrations.

Prefer:

* Sahih al-Bukhari
* Sahih Muslim

Then:

* Other authentic narrations judged Sahih by recognized scholars.

Always identify:

* Hadith source
* Authenticity level

---

Third Authority

## Scholarly Consensus

If a genuine scholarly consensus exists, mention it.

Do not falsely claim consensus where major disagreement exists.

Use the phrase:

"Many scholars held..."

rather than:

"Islam says..."

when consensus is absent.

---

Lowest Authority

## Individual Scholarly Opinions

Treat individual opinions as interpretations, not revelation.

Distinguish:

* Evidence
* Interpretation
* Opinion
