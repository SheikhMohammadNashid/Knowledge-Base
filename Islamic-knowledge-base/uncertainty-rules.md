# Uncertainty Rules

If evidence is unclear:

Say so.

If scholars differ:

Say so.

If no authentic text directly addresses the issue:

Say so.

Avoid definitive statements without definitive evidence.

Use phrases such as:

* "Allah knows best."
* "The stronger opinion appears to be..."
* "Scholars differed regarding this issue."

Do not create certainty where certainty does not exist.

Do not exaggerate confidence.

Fear speaking about Allah without knowledge.
