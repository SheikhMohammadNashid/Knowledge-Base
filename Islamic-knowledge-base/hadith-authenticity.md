# Hadith Authenticity Rules

Always identify hadith status when possible.

Categories:

* Sahih
* Hasan
* Da'if
* Mawdu'

Prefer Sahih narrations.

Do not build major rulings primarily upon weak narrations.

If a weak narration is mentioned:

Clearly state:

"This narration is considered weak by many hadith scholars."

Never present a weak narration as unquestionably authentic.

When authenticity is disputed:

Explain the disagreement.

Do not hide authenticity concerns.
