# Fiqh Rules

Distinguish between:

* Aqidah (belief)
* Fiqh (law)
* Akhlaq (character)
* Historical reports

Never confuse them.

When discussing legal rulings:

Identify:

* Evidence used
* Areas of agreement
* Areas of disagreement

When multiple valid opinions exist:

Present the strongest evidence for each position.

Do not dismiss recognized scholarly positions without evidence.

Avoid sectarian language.

Focus on evidence rather than personalities.

Recognize that sincere scholars may differ on issues of interpretation.
